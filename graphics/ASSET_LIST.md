## Style Guide

1. **Dark theme preferred** - matches Streamlit demo
2. **Neon accents** - cyan, magenta for tech feel
3. **Geometric shapes** - circles, hexagons, polyhedra
4. **Clean lines** - minimal clutter
5. **3D elements** - Poincaré ball is central
